# ComplianceLM - Multi-Agent GRC Engine

**Research. Risk. Compliance. Intelligence.**

ComplianceLM is a multi-agent Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) intelligence platform that leverages specialized AI personas to analyze regulations, policies, audit reports, cybersecurity frameworks, and enterprise documents.

The platform generates evidence-backed compliance assessments, cybersecurity audits, risk registers, executive intelligence reports, board-ready presentations, and regulatory gap analyses with traceable source citations.

## Key Capabilities

* Multi-Agent AI Analysis
* Regulatory Compliance Assessment
* ISO 27001 Gap Analysis
* SAMA Compliance Review
* Cybersecurity Audits
* Risk Register Generation
* Executive Intelligence Summaries
* Board Presentation Generation
* Evidence-Based Citations & Traceability
* Arabic & English Interface
* Secure Client-Side Document Processing

## Core Value Proposition

Transform complex regulations, policies, cybersecurity frameworks, and enterprise documentation into actionable compliance intelligence through specialized AI agents and evidence-backed analysis.

## Innovation

ComplianceLM goes beyond traditional document chat by introducing specialized AI compliance agents that analyze the same source material from different professional perspectives. The platform enables executive, audit, cybersecurity, risk, and regulatory intelligence from a single document repository while maintaining full traceability through source-backed citations.
